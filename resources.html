Resources for Physical Therapy

Explore these resources to enhance your physical therapy experience and support your recovery journey.

Educational Materials

  • Physio-pedia - A comprehensive online resource for physical therapy professionals and patients.
  • PT Today - News and resources for physical therapy and rehabilitation.
  • Healthline Physical Therapy Guide - An informative guide on physical therapy techniques and benefits.

Support Services

  • American Physical Therapy Association (APTA) - A professional organization offering resources and support for physical therapists.
  • NHS Physiotherapy - Information on physical therapy services available through the NHS.
  • American College of Rheumatology - Resources on physical therapy for managing rheumatic conditions.

Tools and Apps

  • MyTherapy App - A mobile app to help you track and manage your physical therapy exercises.
  • Sworkit - Offers customized workout plans including physical therapy exercises.
  • Pzizz - Sleep and relaxation app that can complement your recovery process.

Books and Publications

  • Physical Therapy Book for Patients - A guide to understanding physical therapy and exercises.
  • Modern Physical Therapy Textbook - A comprehensive textbook for students and practitioners.
