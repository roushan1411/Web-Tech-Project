Staying Motivated

Tips for Staying Motivated

  • Setting Realistic Goals: Break down your overall goal into smaller, achievable steps. Celebrate each milestone you reach.
  • Creating a Routine: Establish a regular exercise schedule and stick to it. Consistency is key to seeing progress.
  • Finding Support: Engage with family, friends, or a physical therapy group for encouragement and accountability.
  • Celebrating Milestones: Reward yourself for reaching goals, no matter how small. It keeps you motivated and focused.
